About Swift Reader App

Read two to three times faster with Swift Reader App. Use Swift Reader App, the top rated and most popular speed reading application of its kind, to read text in your browser faster.

You can use Swift Reader App to speed read whatever text you can copy-paste into Swift Reader App. Swift Reader App works by utilizing a visual technique called Rapid Serial Visual Presentation, or RSVP, used by the fastest speed readers in the world.

Swift Reader App helps you eliminate your inner voice that makes each word known as "sub-vocalization" and focus too much on the words themselves so that you can read more visually, which means reading faster while understanding. The average person reads 200 words per minute with Swift Reader App, you can easily train yourself to read at twice the speed, 400 words per minute or faster.

Speed Reader App can be used to teach children how to read at a beginners level as well as help people with dyslexia to read at a faster rate.

Swift Reader App is always in development. Send me an email at swiftreader911@gmail.com with any bugs or features you would find helpful.
